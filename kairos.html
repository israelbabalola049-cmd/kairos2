Features

AI Health Tracker

Kairos analyzes your daily habits and wellness signals using adaptive AI. It turns your data into clear insights so you understand your health in a simple, smarter way.

Workout Planner

The Kairos Workout Planner creates routines based on your goals, energy, and progress. It adjusts automatically, helping you train consistently without overthinking.

Mood & Habit Tracking

Kairos tracks your moods alongside your habits to show how your lifestyle affects your mindset. It helps you understand your patterns and build healthier routines.

Smart Reminders

Smart Reminders trigger when you're most likely to respond, adapting to your schedule and mood. They keep you on track without stressing or interrupting your flow.
